About This Tour

Step into a serene space that honors the life and legacy of Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation. Raj Ghat marks the exact site where Gandhi was cremated in 1948. Surrounded by lush gardens and a tranquil atmosphere, this site serves as a solemn reminder of India’s struggle for independence through non-violent resistance. The black marble platform inscribed with “Hey Ram”—Gandhi’s last words—creates a deeply reflective space for visitors to connect with the ideals of peace and tolerance. This experience offers a meaningful start or peaceful break in your Delhi sightseeing.
